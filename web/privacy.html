Squigglebug Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Squigglebug is a drawing app designed for babies and toddlers. We take your child's privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Squigglebug collects (spoiler: none).

Data Collection

Squigglebug does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies
  • No advertising
  • No in-app purchases
  • No camera, microphone, or location access
  • No data shared with third parties

All drawings are temporary and exist only in device memory while the app is open. Nothing is saved or uploaded.

Internet Permission

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely because it is built with Capacitor (a web-to-native framework). Squigglebug does not make any network requests during normal use.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Squigglebug is designed for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date above. Since we collect no data, we don't anticipate changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue at github.com/skolez/squigglebug.
